The appointment of a new Director-General of Police (DGP) is currently the talk of the town in Uttar Pradesh. Meanwhile, in Bihar, the DGP has directed policemen not to use phones on duty. Becoming a DGP is no mean feat and one has to crack the UPSC exam. UPSC exams are taken by aspirants who want to become a part of the IAS or IPS. The Civil Services Exam is one of the toughest tests in the country and only a handful actually pass the exams among the millions who aspire.
